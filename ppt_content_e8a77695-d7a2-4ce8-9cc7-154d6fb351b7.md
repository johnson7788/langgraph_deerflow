## Historical and Cultural Significance

- Nanjing Tangbao dates back to the Ming and Qing Dynasties
- It's one of Nanjing's culinary specialties
- Tourism in Nanjing promotes cultural heritage protection

---

## Characteristics and Ingredients

- Thin, almost translucent skin
- Generous amount of rich broth
- Savory filling with minced pork and seasonings
- Key ingredients: minced pork, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, sesame oil, Shaoxing wine, aspic

---

## Preparation

- Marinate the pork
- Make the aspic
- Prepare the dough for wrappers
- Fill the dumplings
- Steam them
- High - quality pork is crucial

---

## Modern Adaptations and Variations

- Some consider modern Xiao Long Bao as Nanjing Tangbao
- Nanjing's Tangbao is larger than Shanghai’s Xiaolongbao, with more broth, often served with a straw

---

## Notable Establishments

- Yinshi Jishi Tangbao at No. 398 Mochou Road
- Fuzimiao (Confucius Temple) and Hunan Road
- Zhiwei Guan, Zhu Yansheng Tang Bao
- Liu Changxing for savory dumplings

---

## Impact of Tourism

- Promotes local transportation, hotels, and retail stores
- Increases popularity and availability of Nanjing Tangbao
- Contributes to cultural heritage protection and related industry growth
